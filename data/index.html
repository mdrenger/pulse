Open Data

Pulse is a project of the General Services Administration that measures how U.S. government domains are following best practices for federal websites and it produces a lot of data in the process.

You can download that data below for reuse. It's not required, but if you use this data to do something interesting, we'd love it if you opened a ticket or dropped us a line to tell us about it!
